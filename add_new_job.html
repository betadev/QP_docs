Add New Jobs

A job consists of a set of operations & parameters that are defined in a particular process plan (model). Each job can be tracked by it's unique QR label or serial number. Jobs can also be linked to other jobs or sub - jobs (in case of assemblies with many components) in order to comprehensively track all components of a job.


Creating / Adding a New Job

  • Click the Add New Job button on the app's main screen.
  • Enter a Serial Number for the job you want to track. (Note - Serial Number cannot contain '/')
  • Select a Model type for the job.
  • Click continue and select 'Yes' in the confirmation dialog.
  • After successfuly adding the job, you can download or print it's QR label.

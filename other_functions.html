Installing Qik Process App

  • Android
    • Chrome / Firefox / Samsung Internet
      • Tap on the 3 vertical dots () to open the options menu in your browser.
      • Select "Add to Home Screen" from the option list and follow the prompts.
    • Other Info
      • Ensure that your browser is in Regular Mode and not in Private Browsing Mode
      • For other browsers, follow similar steps as above and select "Add to Home Screen" to install the app on your phone.
  • ios :
    • Safari Browser
      • Click the share button in your browser.
      • Scroll down (if needed) and select the "Add to Home Screen" Button

Changing / Resetting Account Password

  • Go to the Qik Process Login page & click on the Reset Password button.
  • Enter your email id and click "Reset Password".
  • Follow the password reset instructions present in the email you receive.

Managing Credits

This sections lets you view current credit balance, check usage history and purchase more credits. Credits are required for the following actions:

  • Adding New Jobs : 1 credit per serial number generated
  • Generating Reports : 1 credit per 200 records fetched in a report
  • Operation Disruption Alerts : 1 credit per 5 people subscribed for disruption alerts of a particular operation
Tip : Try out these actions in our Demo App & check credit usage report to see how it works.

Checking Current Credit Balance and Average Credit Usage

  • Click the View Credits button on the app's main screen.
  • Your current credit balance and average daily credit usage will be displayed.

Checking Detailed Credit Usage History

  • Click the View Credits button on the app's main screen.
  • Click the "Download Usage Statement" button.
  • Your usage statement displays your credit purchase and usage summary as well as detailed history. The statement contains info for the last 3 months and the current month.

Purchasing Credits

  • Click the View Credits button on the app's main screen.
  • Click "Buy More Credits" in the Credit Balance tab.
  • Fill in the required info on the order page and select how many credits you want to purchase.
Note : On successful order completion, you will receive a payment receipt on your email. Final invoice will be sent in 1 to 3 working days after payment confirmation on the same email.

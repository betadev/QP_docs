Edit / Update Jobs

This section lets you view, enter or update a job's parameters at different operation stages. You can also view change logs, parameter deviations, operation statuses and download the job report.

Editing / Updating Job Data
  • Click the Update Job Status button on the app's main screen.
  • Scan the job's QR label or enter it's serial number and click Continue.
  • Navigate to the required Operation stage using the dropdown menu on top or the navigation arrows.
  • Scroll down and click the Edit Data button (user permission needed).
  • Enter all parameter values. A red highlight indicates the value is incorrect or not as per specifications.
  • Click Save / Update Data button.
Allowing Deviation / Rejecting a Job
  • Click the Update Job Status button on the app's main screen.
  • Scan the job's QR label or enter it's serial number and click Continue.
  • Navigate to the required Operation stage using the dropdown menu on top or the navigation arrows.
  • Scroll down and click the 'Allow Deviation' or 'Reject Part' button as required (user permission needed).
  • Enter your remarks and click Yes.

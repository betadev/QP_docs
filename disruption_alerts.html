Process Disruption Alerts

This section lets you view / report / close disruptions reported for any operations.

Reporting a Disruption
  • Click the Process Disruption button on the app's main screen.
  • Select 'Report Process Disruption' tab.
  • Select Disruption Reason, Operation & Workstation and click the 'Report Process Disruption' button.
Note: In-app & email alert notifications will be sent to all users subscribed to receive alerts (in user settings) for that operation
View active Disruptions
  • Click the Process Disruption button on the app's main screen.
  • Select 'View Process Disruptions' tab.
Closing / Fixing a Disruption
  • Click the Process Disruption button on the app's main screen.
  • Select 'View Process Disruptions' tab.
  • Scroll to the required operation disruption listing & click the 'Disruption Fixed' button.
